Daffy Darja demo: Wilaya input

Suppose you are Walae, a 20yo from Constantine. You were browsing entries in the Daffy Darja app and stumbled upon this. To add a translation in your dialect, you simply type it and hit enter (or click 'Add'). You can also add translations in other dialects by prefixing the text with the corresponding wilaya's number or abbreviation. For example, 21: Faynk? or SK: Faynk?"
